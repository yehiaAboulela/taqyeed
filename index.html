ABOUT US

WHO WE ARE ?

Taqyeed, meaning “ Registration “in Arabic, is a legal service center providing a variety of services to the public in one place. With the help of trained and qualified Emirati employees, trained by Ali Al Khajeh Advocate & Legal Consultants & Private Notary who are experts in the legal field, we aim to improve the efficiency of legal processes and services. We are dedicated to providing legal services for businesses, private matters and government.

services we provide

Through a long-term partnership with Ali Al Khajeh Advocate & Legal Consultants, Taqyeed is able to strategically provide a seamless experience for our customers and a wide range of legal services such as:

Services of the Department of Municipalities and Transport “Abu Dhabi Municipality”

Taqyeed completes land, advertising, food and beverage, construction, entertainment, pet, professional certifications and contracts licenses and approvals.

Judicial Department transactions “online”

Movables, commercial premises, sale of shares and boats, sole proprietorship, sale, assignment, work, service agent and its annexes, commercial contracts, reconciliation, settlement agreements and coalition contracts.

Declarations services

Complete all transactions of the Judicial Department (registration of cases - notary public and documentation - implementation - prosecution - appeals), transactions for inquiring about a case, following up on the case, and inquiring about cases.

Clearing transactions

We handle all transactions on your behalf with a team of the most skilled experts in clearing transactions in all government departments in the UAE.

Ministry of Community Development services “People of Determination Card”

The service of issuing the People of Determination card, which is granted by the Ministry of Community Development as an official document proving that its holder is a person with a disability, to guarantee his rights and services, will be terminated.

Health insurance services “Daman”

Health insurance package services, applying for, enjoying and obtaining them inside and outside the UAE, renewing insurance policies and “Daman” services.

Zakat Fund services

We perform all Zakat Fund services, such as requesting a fatwa, calculating Zakat, paying it, and everything related to requesting Zakat, following it up, asking for forgiveness, and serving major benefactors.

Red Crescent services “Aid”

We provide Red Crescent services, including aid, health care, emergency intervention teams, restoring family links, community and urban development, slums, income activities and projects.

Submitting job applications to all entities

Submitting job applications to all government agencies and departments and the private sector and providing the necessary support in following up on jobs, their advertisements and competitions.

All types of visas for all foreign countries

We complete the procedures for obtaining visas for all foreign countries and submit papers, documents and applications to obtain those visas.

Services for reducing traffic violations and fines

We provide requests for early payment of traffic violations and their reduction services, payment of fines and submission of their requests, reserved car services, and the reduction of traffic fines.

Traffic and licensing services

We terminate traffic services, all types of licenses, the traffic file, vehicles and their licenses, and anything related to their services and traffic violations.

Residency violations

Taqyeed ends all procedures related to residency violations, submits its requests for clemency, and takes procedures to pay its violations without any burdens borne by the person who is exposed to residency violations.

Health insurance violations

Taqeed carries out all procedures related to health insurance violations, on behalf of individuals and companies for the benefit of their employees, as well as paying fines for late renewal of insurance and related procedures.

Other additional services

We provide additional services such as corporate services, multinational corporate dispute resolution, business services, mediation, debt collection, printing, photocopying, legal translation, and drafting and reviewing international contracts.
